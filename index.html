About me

I am currently a postdoc at the Program in Applied and Computational Mathematics (PACM) of Princeton University. My research interests are broadly in machine learning theory and applying machine learning to solve scientific computing problems.

I completed my Ph.D. in computational mathematics at Peking University in 2018, advised by Prof. Weinan E. Before that, I received the B.S. degree in pure mathematics from Nankai University in 2012.
